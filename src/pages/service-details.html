How to buy a house: A step-by-step guide

Buying a home is an exciting & often adventurous process. We've created a step-by-step guide to help you understand the nuances of the home-buying.

Make sure you are ready:

If you're looking to buy your first home, start by determining whether you're ready to make a long-term commitment.

Find a real estate agent:

Working with a good real estate agent who understands the local market, will stick within your budget, and can guide you

Purchase homeowners insurance:

Even though you don't own the home, often you will need to take out homeowners insurance to get the lender to finalize the loan.

Make an offer and negotiate:

Once you find a property you like, make an offer to the seller or landlord. Negotiate terms like price, lease period for rent.

Finalize the contract:

Review all terms and conditions thoroughly, ensuring clarity & understanding. Sign the contract and make the payment.
